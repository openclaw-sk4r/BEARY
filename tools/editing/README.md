# BEARY Editing Tool

**Refine and polish your existing writing—without changing your voice.**

---

## 🚧 Coming Soon

The Editing Tool is currently in development. This tool will help you improve your writing while maintaining your unique voice and message.

---

## 🎯 Planned Features

### What It Will Do
- **Clarity improvements** - Suggest ways to make your writing clearer
- **Flow optimization** - Identify awkward transitions or structure issues
- **Consistency checking** - Catch inconsistencies in terminology or style
- **Strengthening suggestions** - Highlight areas that could be more impactful

### What It Won't Do
- ❌ Rewrite your content
- ❌ Change your voice or tone
- ❌ Generate new ideas or content
- ❌ Make decisions about what to say

---

## 💡 Philosophy

The Editing Tool is designed around a core principle: **your writing is yours**.

Unlike tools that rewrite or generate content, this tool will:
- ✅ Suggest improvements, not replacements
- ✅ Preserve your voice and style
- ✅ Enhance what you've already written
- ✅ Help you become a better writer

---

## 🔮 Planned Workflow

```
1. Provide your written content
2. Specify what you want to improve (clarity, flow, consistency, etc.)
3. Review suggestions and apply what makes sense
4. Keep your voice, improve your impact
```

## 🤔 Why This Tool Matters

Many AI writing tools focus on generation or rewriting. The Editing Tool takes a different approach:

**Traditional AI Writing Tools:**
- Generate content from prompts
- Rewrite entire sections
- May lose your unique voice
- Can feel impersonal

**BEARY Editing Tool:**
- Works with YOUR content
- Suggests targeted improvements
- Preserves your voice
- Helps you grow as a writer
